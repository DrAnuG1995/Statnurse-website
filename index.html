Why Skip the Agency?

Most agency nurses still go through an agency that takes a cut, controls the conversation, and slows everything down. StatNurse cuts the agency out and connects you straight to the facility.

The Old Way: Through an Agency

  • Agency takes 20-35% of your rate
  • Endless calls, texts, and chasing recruiters
  • Hidden conditions revealed only after you commit
  • No way to compare wards or facilities before you go
  • Restrictive contracts and exclusivity clauses
  • Slow, opaque payment cycles

The StatNurse Way

  • Higher rates, no agency commission
  • Browse and apply on your own time, in-app
  • Transparent rates, ward, and shift details upfront
  • Real reviews from other agency nurses
  • No exclusivity. Work where you want, when you want
  • Direct, fast payments from the facility

Founder, StatDoctor and StatNurse

I started StatDoctor after seeing first-hand how broken agency work had become in Australia. Agencies were controlling every conversation, hiding rates, and frustrating hospitals with the same middlemen. So I built the platform I wished existed: doctors and hospitals talking directly, on transparent terms.

Today, 300+ doctors and 60+ healthcare facilities use StatDoctor. Every shift posted goes straight from the hospital to the doctor, with no agency, no commission, and no chasing recruiters. Nurses kept asking for the same thing, so we're building it. StatNurse launches December 2026, and you can be one of the first nurses on the platform.
